# Currency Futures Trading Guide

Trading in currency pairs like USDINR, EURINR, GBPINR, and JPYINR.

## Charge Breakdown

### 1. Brokerage
- **Rate**: 0.03% or Rs. 20 per executed order.

### 2. STT/CTT
- **Rate**: Zero. No STT is charged on currency trades in India.

### 3. Transaction Charges
- **NSE**: 0.0009%
- **BSE**: 0.00022%

# Commodity Futures Trading Guide

Trading in metals, energy, and agri-products on MCX.

## Charge Breakdown

### 1. Brokerage
- **Rate**: 0.03% or Rs. 20 per order.

### 2. CTT (Commodities Transaction Tax)
- **Rate**: 0.01% on the Sell side (Non-agri).

### 3. Transaction Charges
- **MCX**: 0.0026% (varies by product group).

# Commodity Options Trading Guide

## Charge Breakdown

### 2. CTT
